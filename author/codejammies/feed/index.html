http://codejammies.wordpress.com/?p=573 Read More]]> My then-boyfriend, now-husband and I have been together for 4 years and just like every couple we have spoiled each other with tons of presents. From simple handmade and/or expensive store-bought gifts to couple’s spa sessions we have done everything! So lately it’s been a real challenge finding something interesting and I am sure we are not alone. Just a couple months back we celebrated our first wedding anniversary and like a good wife I spent hours on the internet looking for a perfect present. I kept looking and looking hopping from one site to another with no luck. But just when I was about to give up and settle for an ordinary gift, an ad popped up on my Instagram feed and swept me off my feet! There it was Etsy again saving me yet another time. Honestly, I have been a big fan of Etsy for quite some time because it never disappoints if you are into uncommon finds. So without wasting more time, let’s dig right in!

***To view the product, click on the picture. To view the store, click on the store name.

Wooden Street Cutouts from 1Man1Garage

Of course I am going to start with the present I gave my husband on our anniversary! These guys literally saved me during my desperate times- I was frustrated and running short on time but they were kind enough to squeeze me in and got my personalized order ready in just 2 days! Their wooden street cutouts and city maps are to die for and also come in different sizes. How cool is that?

HD Wood Portrait Pallet from Rawkrft

This one is my personal favorite. Funny story- I was a bit skeptical when I ordered this for the first time but when it arrived…hallelujah! It turned out SO good. Rich look and great picture quality. I liked it so much that I gifted this to 3 of my friends and they absolutely loved it.

Friends Apartment Door Portrait Frame from PbarkerStudio

Hold on..did someone say F.R.I.E.N.D.S? and then an actual miniature of Monica’s apartment door which also is a legit photo frame?! OHH-MYYY-GODDDD!! Could I be more excited?

Friends Yellow Door Frame from TucsonDream

Yet another great F.R.I.E.N.D.S fanatic find on Etsy. The iconic yellow frame on Monica Geller’s apartment door at a very affordable price!

Handmade Animal Slippers from DarkaYarka

Now hang on I know what you are thinking, animal slippers are neither new nor uncommon but before questioning my find, you got to check these babies out! These are not any regular supermarket animal/poop slippers, these are premium custom-made handmade woollen slippers which actually look pretty convincing. Well done DarkaYarka.

Pressed Flower Jewelry from MARIAELA

I discovered this store sometime in January of this year and have been a fan ever since. She makes the most beautiful handmade jewelry and not just with pressed flowers but also sand, glass, stones and yes of course metal. I bought a beautiful set of wolf earrings (big GOT fan) and an electroformed mother of pearl moon statement ring and I am absolutely happy with my purchase.

Actual Kids Drawing Necklace from CaitlynMinimalist

These guys will transform your kid’s art into necklaces! But of course doodles not limited to just kids, you can also print one of your drawings or doodles and flaunt it around your neck. Freeze your special doodles for-eva.

The Office Illustration Magnet from thefoundretail

Again one of the many things I ordered from Etsy. You can find some really awesome pins, magnets, stickers and tags here at a very affordable price! P.S. this store is in my favorites.

Came across more uncommon finds? Comment below and let me know!

I hope you enjoyed this read. Until next time!

Today it’s been 14 days and I am going c-r-a-z-y! Just kidding. To be completely honest with you all, the introvert in me is subtly happy with this change in routine. After a long time I am getting some quality time with my thoughts, I have enough time to get my work and chores done and I am still left with a decent amount of time to spend on myself.

I know it is not easy to stay locked in, and it is even harder when you are forced to do so (especially by the government!). But let’s take a moment here to appreciate the time that we have gotten. Remember all those times when we wished we had some more time? Well, there you go. This is an opportunity to do everything that you ever wanted to do by staying at home. You are basically an adult who is grounded. So why not embrace it by doing whatever the heck you planned on doing but never got a chance? Doesn’t even need to be productive. Get your 8 hours sleep, cook fresh-eat fresh, get creative in the kitchen, catch up with your friends and family virtually, get to know your partner better, play board games, start a blog, brush up/learn a new skill or just Netflix and chill! Or just count the number of times I used the word “time” throughout this article…pff!

It is heartbreaking how the world is going down with COVID-19. I died a little inside when I heard how Italy doesn’t have enough space to bury their dead. Today US tested more than 100k virus affected people. It is a sad sad time for humanity and it feels more depressing when you are facing it alone…stuck alone without a friend, family or a companion. But let’s not forget that we are in this together, mentally and spiritually if not physically. It is challenging to workout or go for a walk when you have such limited access to the outside world, but meditation, yoga or stretching can be your buddies. 10 minutes of daily meditation session can help calm your nerves. I found this beautiful meditation music on the internet today and there are many more to choose from according to your taste.

I remember the very first time I meditated, I was 14 and had no clue how shavasana worked, I legit fell asleep. My guru sat next to me and clapped his hands loudly. I woke up like a deer in the headlights thinking what the heck happened and the next thing I knew all the kids in my camp were laughing their heads off. Well, jokes on you suckers I got an extra hour of sleep that day paa-haa! Jokes apart, meditation has some great benefits and I am just relaying my experience. Whenever I have meditated I have felt calmer, happier, focused and more confident. But despite such benefits my lazy ass still discontinued this habit. May be I will get in touch with it again, may be I will document my journey, who knows? At least I have the time to consider it and so do you! Here are some beginner’s meditation videos that I found on YouTube.

You are probably bored sitting home and watching the same Netflix shows on repeat, but on the brighter side I can actually hear birds chirping outside my window everyday for an unbelievably long time, I heard that people spotted peacocks roaming on streets in Madrid and dolphins near Marine Drive, Mumbai. Lowered pollution and significant improvement in air quality in number of places. How beautiful is that? Imagine how cool it would be to spot a peacock while heading to work? Might raise a risk of getting into accidents more often but eh what’s one broken arm and a dented trunk for one good Instagram post huh? Just kidding!

It’s almost 1 AM and I am getting real sleepy so before I call it a day, here are the famous do the 5 to help stop coronavirus-

Hands – Wash them often

Elbow – Cough into it

Face – Don’t touch it

Space – Keep safe distance

Home – Stay if you can
