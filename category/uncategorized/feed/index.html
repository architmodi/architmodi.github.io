I’m Sally, your author, and I’m happy to announce that Codejammies is 4 years old. We at Codejammies have decided to rebrand our blog. We’ve started a lifestyle blog, which is just an extension of Codejammies. It’s called Spill The Tea and is officially online! We are working on our domain (we got the domain but we are still fixing a few issues- that being said, you are welcome to check it out here), but until then, feel free to check out our fresh articles here. It’s safe to say that we will be actively using Wix until we iron out our domain issues.

We at Spill The Tea are committed to providing positive, insightful, and original articles covering travel, food, health, relationships, styling, and editorial observations. And of course, tech. A part of Spill The Tea is still dedicated in bringing awareness in gender bias in tech and empowering women to join STEM. Just like Codejammies, we love to hear and collaborate with our readers!

Spill The Tea already has 4 articles up for your enjoyment and we look forward to hearing your thoughts on them. Thank you for the love and support for Codejammies, we appreciate each and every like, share, collaboration, and support in of any and every kind, and we hope to see your love on our new blog.

With love,

Sally
