About Me

Hey there! Thanks for stopping by. I am a motivated young engineer working in tech industry. I am passionate about troubleshooting problems and spreading knowledge. I feel strongly about women pushing the limits to succeed in their careers and would do everything in my capacity to encourage more women in STEM. Other than that I love arts, museums, traveling and listening to people’s stories.

My background- Undergrad in Electronics, Masters in Network Engineering, and now working in a great IT company.

Skills- Linux, Telecom Networking, Website Development, Computer Architecture.

It will warm my heart if I manage to help at least one person with my articles. Share your knowledge/thoughts through comments and help me get better. After all, these are just some lessons between us dummies.
